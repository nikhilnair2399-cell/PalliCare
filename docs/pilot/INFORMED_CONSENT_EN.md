## PARTICIPANT INFORMATION SHEET

### 1. Introduction

You are being invited to take part in a research study. Before you decide whether to participate, it is important for you to understand why the research is being done and what it will involve. Please take time to read the following information carefully. Ask us if there is anything that is not clear or if you would like more information. Take time to decide whether or not you wish to take part.

### 2. Purpose of the Study

We are evaluating a mobile phone application called **"PalliCare"** that has been developed to help palliative care patients manage their pain and other symptoms. The app allows you to:

- Record your pain level and other symptoms on your phone
- Track your medication schedule and confirm when you have taken your medicines
- Practice breathing exercises for relaxation
- Learn about managing your condition

The purpose of this study is to find out if the PalliCare app is:

- **Easy to use** for patients and their caregivers
- **Helpful** in keeping track of pain and symptoms between clinic visits
- **Useful** in helping your care team understand your symptoms better
- **Technically reliable** and safe to use

This is a pilot study involving approximately 30 patients and 15 caregivers at AIIMS Bhopal over a period of 4 weeks.

### 3. Why Have I Been Chosen?

You have been invited because you are receiving palliative care at AIIMS Bhopal, you have access to an Android smartphone (your own or a caregiver's), and your treating doctor believes you may benefit from using this application.

### 4. What Will Participation Involve?

If you agree to participate, you will be asked to do the following:

**At enrollment (1 visit, approximately 45 minutes):**
1. Sign this consent form
2. Install the PalliCare app on your Android smartphone (or your caregiver's phone)
3. Attend a brief training session (30 minutes) on how to use the app

**Over the next 4 weeks (at home):**
4. Record your pain and symptoms using the app at least 3 times per week (each entry takes 1-5 minutes)
5. Confirm your medication intake daily using the app's medication tracker
6. Use any other features of the app as you wish (breathing exercises, educational content)

**At the end of 4 weeks (1 visit, approximately 30 minutes):**
7. Complete a usability questionnaire about your experience with the app (10 minutes)
8. Optionally, participate in a brief interview about your experience (20 minutes)

**Total time commitment**: Approximately 5-10 minutes per day for app use, plus 2 study visits.

### 5. What Data Will Be Collected?

The following data will be collected through the app:

| Data Type | What It Includes | How It Is Used |
|-----------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Symptom logs** | Pain score (0-10), pain location, mood, sleep quality, other symptoms | To track your symptoms over time and share with your care team |
| **Medication logs** | Which medicines you took and when, PRN (as-needed) medication use | To help monitor medication adherence and inform your care team |
| **App usage data** | How often you use the app, which features you use, time spent | To understand how easy the app is to use (this data is anonymized) |
| **Survey responses** | Your opinions about the app's usability and usefulness | To evaluate and improve the app |

**Important**: Your personal information (name, phone number) is stored separately from your health data and will not be included in any published results.

### 6. What Are the Possible Risks?

The risks of participating in this study are minimal:

- **Time**: Using the app will require 5-10 minutes per day
- **Emotional**: Recording your symptoms may sometimes remind you of your illness, which could cause mild distress. The app includes breathing exercises that may help. You can skip any entry at any time.
- **Privacy**: Your health information will be stored securely on your phone and on a secure server. We take extensive measures to protect your privacy (see Section 9 below).
- **Technical**: The app may occasionally have minor issues or require updates. A support team is available to help.

**IMPORTANT**: The PalliCare app is NOT a substitute for your regular medical care. It does not diagnose conditions, prescribe medicines, or provide medical advice.
- **In any medical emergency, call 112 immediately** or go to the nearest emergency department.
- **For palliative care concerns**, contact your treating doctor or the AIIMS Bhopal palliative care helpline.

### 7. What Are the Possible Benefits?

By participating, you may benefit from:

- **Better symptom tracking**: Understanding your pain patterns and triggers over time
- **Improved communication**: Your care team will have more detailed information about your symptoms between visits
- **Medication reminders**: Timely notifications to help you take your medicines on schedule
- **Self-management tools**: Access to breathing exercises and educational content about pain management
- **Contributing to research**: Helping develop a tool that may benefit future palliative care patients in India

There is no guarantee of direct clinical benefit from participation.

### 8. Do I Have to Take Part?

**No. Participation is entirely voluntary.**

- You are free to refuse to participate without giving any reason.
- If you agree to participate, you may withdraw from the study at any time, without giving a reason and without your medical care being affected in any way.
- If you withdraw, you may request that all your data be deleted from the study database.
- You may skip any survey question or app entry that you do not wish to complete.

**Your decision to participate or not will have NO effect on the quality of medical care you receive at AIIMS Bhopal.**

### 9. Confidentiality and Data Protection

We take your privacy very seriously. The following measures are in place to protect your data:

**In accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023:**

1. **Encryption**: All your health data is encrypted both on your phone and when transmitted to the server, using industry-standard encryption (AES-256 and TLS 1.3).
2. **Access control**: Only authorized members of the research team can access your identifiable data. Clinicians can only see data for patients they are treating.
3. **Anonymization**: App usage analytics are stripped of all personal identifying information before analysis. Published results will only contain anonymized, aggregate data.
4. **Data minimization**: We collect only the data necessary for the study. The app does not access your contacts, photos, location, or any other personal data on your phone.
5. **Data retention**: Your clinical data will be retained for 6 months after the study ends for analysis purposes. After this period, data will be reviewed for archival or permanent deletion.
6. **No third-party sharing**: Your data will not be shared with any third party, commercial entity, or insurance company.

**Your rights under DPDPA 2023:**
- You have the right to access all personal data collected about you.
- You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate data.
- You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time.
- You have the right to receive your data in a portable format.

### 10. Compensation

There is no direct monetary payment for participating in this study. A token travel reimbursement of **INR 200 per study-related visit** (beyond your routine clinic visits) may be provided to cover transportation costs.

### 11. What Happens to the Results?

The results of this study will be:
- Reported to AIIMS Bhopal's Department of Anaesthesiology & Palliative Medicine
- Potentially published in a medical journal (all data will be anonymized)
- Presented at medical conferences
- Used to improve the PalliCare application for future patients

You will not be personally identified in any report or publication.

### 12. Who Has Reviewed This Study?

This study has been reviewed and approved by the **Institutional Ethics Committee (IEC) of AIIMS Bhopal**, which ensures that research involving patients meets ethical standards and protects participants' rights.
